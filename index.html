CoreWar Reference

What is CoreWar?

CoreWar is a programming game where warriors — programs written in Redcode assembly — battle inside a virtual computer's memory (the "core"). Each warrior tries to survive while crashing opponents by forcing them to execute a DAT instruction, which kills the process.

Strategies

Imp — Copies itself forward endlessly. Simple but surprisingly effective as a tie-breaker.
Bomber — Drops DAT bombs at regular intervals to crash anything in its path.
Scanner — Searches for non-empty cells before attacking. Precise but slower.
Replicator — Creates copies of itself. Hard to kill since it exists in multiple places.
Vampire — Plants JMP traps to redirect enemy processes to a "pit".
